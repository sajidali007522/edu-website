Privacy Statement

Introduction

We from SOFY GmbH, office park Donau Inkustraße 1-7, 3400 Klosterneuburg, info@sofy.group, (hereinafter referred to as "SOFY", "we" or "us") have set ourselves the goal of edupression.com® exclusively on the building the foundation of evidence-based medicine. People who are in a difficult time for them, should be given the opportunity to learn online self-help and build expertise, in addition to the existing forms of therapy psychotherapy and psychotropic drugs. Our claim is the science and use of the highest technical level. For this reason, the protection of the personal data of our customers (users) and interested parties is also of particular concern to us in the sensitive area. We process this data solely on the basis of the applicable, strict legal provisions in the European Union and Austria (GDPR, Austrian Dataprotection Law and Telecommunication Law).

Registration and Use of edupression.com®

In order to be able to use edupression.com® as an online platform and medical device, thus on the basis of Art 6 (1) (b) DSGVO, the users must register (name, password, valid email address.) The mandatory introductory module (online user manual) must also be confirmed by the user that there are no urgent suicidal thoughts or plans.

When registering, they must also expressly consent to the processing of personal data, in particular to the processing of their health data in accordance with Article 9 (2) (a) GDPR. The registration is only allowed to users from the age of 18 years.

When using edupression.com®, the following data is processed:

Data that users actively enter: contact information, form input for the status of knowledge, self-tests, health, activities, forums, etc
Data that can be detected passively via the (mobile) terminals (motion and acceleration sensors, user behavior, GPS data).
These data are the basis for the application and efficacy of the products of edupression.com® as medical devices.

Health data - use of medical devices

For the purpose of use as medical devices, we process the following health data. This is necessary to be able to guarantee the effect of the product.

edupression.com Psychoeducation (Courses)

Data in the course of the course management (start/end dates of the course modules and their parts, time course, learning time, repetitions, etc.) Data from the self-test on depression (queries about complaints regarding various states of mind, biological functions, feelings and thoughts) Data from brain training to reaction time, concentration and attention Data from interactive fields in the course modules (e.g., term associations, word rap, personal positive activity list, answers from questionnaires, etc.) Data from the user-given quiz answers

edupression.com Mood Chart

Data from daily mood to build a depression score, affective episodes, impairments, general factors such as sleep, menstruation, alcohol / drugs, stressful events, special factors such as postive activities, sports, therapy.

User behavior - captured by end devices

Data from the sensors of user devices like mobile, tablets, laptop (movement, use, GPS)

Use with a depression specialist (therapist)

The user can voluntarily connect to a depression specialist (therapist) to share data from medical device.

To link users and therapist both have to explicitly agree (invitation and consent). When the two are connected, the practitioner has access to data from the user's cockpit, e.g. course history, activities, mood chart. This is to inform the therapist about the period between the personal treatments. Further data from linking a user with a practitioner: master data, data from the communication between the therapist and the user.

The therapist can form his own protected groups among his connected users, which the user must also explicitly agree with. Only the user name is displayed to the group and the data actively communicated by the user, e.g. in the protected group forum.

Use of moderated forums

Names, dates and posts of the user in the login protected, moderated forums

Use under a health insurance

If use is made via insurance, data will be transmitted primarily to the insurance companies for settlement (name, insurance number, date of birth, period of use of edupression.com, selected product).

The above-mentioned data processing takes place in principle on the basis of Art 6 (1) (b) GDPR, however, as far as health data are concerned, the data processing takes place on the basis of Art. 9 (2) (a) GDPR.

The user can revoke his consent at any time without giving reasons, but than he can use edupression.com then only very limited, for example, can not open courses or the mood chart can no longer be used (no more data entry possible)

Collaboration with science partners and sharing of anonymo s data

In R&D we work together with medical-scientific research institutions. Data required for science are transmitted only in an anonymous form, so that the researchers can not draw any conclusions about the person of the individual user.

Our common goal is to further develop edupression.com® in such a way that our users receive the best possible support and support in their illness.

Website - Cookies and Analysis Service
Our website uses so-called cookies. These are small text files that are stored on your device using the browser. They do no harm.

We use cookies to make our offer user-friendly and our web service works technically. Some cookies remain stored on your device until you delete them. They allow us to recognize your browser on your next visit i.e. to make our web service faster available.

If you do not want this, you can set up your browser so that it informs you about the setting of cookies and you allow this only in individual cases.

When deactivating cookies, the functionality of our website and our web shop may be limited.

Web Analytics: Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google Inc. ("Google"). Google uses cookies. The information generated by the cookie about the use of the online offer by the users are usually transmitted to a Google server in the USA and stored there.

Google will use this information on our behalf to evaluate the use of our online offer by users, to compile reports on the activities within this online offer and to provide us with further services related to the use of this online offer and the internet usage. In this case, pseudonymous user profiles of the processed data can be created.

We only use Google Analytics with activated IP anonymization. This means that the IP address of the users is shortened by Google within member states of the European Union or in other contracting states of the Agreement on the European Economic Area. Only in exceptional cases will the full IP address be sent to a Google server in the US and shortened there.

The IP address submitted by the user's browser will not be merged with other data provided by Google. Users can prevent the storage of cookies by setting their browser software accordingly; Users may also prevent the collection by Google of the data generated by the cookie and related to its use of the online offer as well as the processing of this data by Google by downloading and installing the browser plug-in available at the following link: http://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout?hl=de.

Use of the Service is based on Google's Privacy Policy https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=en. Google has been certified under the US Privacy Shield and thus complies with the legal terms as a service provider from a third country under EU GDPR.

E-Mail-Newsletter

We send e-mail newsletters to interested parties and our users (customers) and inform them about mental illnesses and our services. The e-mail delivery takes place on basis of the consent of the prospective customer in accordance with Art. 6 (1) (a) GDPR and from the contractual relationship as a customer. The registration takes place in a so-called double-opt-in procedure (the prospective customer receives after the registration an e-mail, in which he is asked for the confirm, only after confirmation the email-newsletter is sent). To carry out the shipment, we use an e-mail marketing service in France, as a processor. For this purpose, only the following data will be transmitted: name, salutation and e-mail address. In the case of shipping, usage data (reports) are also collected; these are used solely for the purpose of improving our newsletter service and thus for safeguarding our legitimate interests pursuant to Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR. If you have given your consent to receive email newsletters in accordance with Art. 6 (1) (a) GDPR, we will store your data until your withdrawal of your consent.

Unlike the newsletters, accompanying, personalized communication via e-mail (reminders, information, congratulations ...) is an important component of our service offered to our users, hence a contractual part of our service (Art. 6 (1) (b)).

In principle, the transmission and processing of personal data takes place only to the extent necessary for fulfilling the contractual relationship or for the implementation of pre-contractual measures (on the basis of Art. 6 (1) (b) GDPR) to fulfill a legal obligation (Art. 6 (1) (c) DSGVO ), to safeguard legitimate interests (Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR) or to assert, exercise or defend legal claims (Art. 9 (2) (f) GDPR) or if the user or interested party gives his express consent (Art. 6 (1) (a) or Art. 9 (2) (a) GDPR).
