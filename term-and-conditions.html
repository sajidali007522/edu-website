SOFY concludes contracts with depression specialists only under application of these GTC - Depression Specialists in the current version. The Depression Specialist commits himself to read these GTC - Depression Specialists carefully before using the products or free products.

2.2 In addition, SOFY concludes contracts with patients of depression specialists (and thus "users" of edupression.com®) under application of the General Terms and Conditions (GTC) of SOFY GmbH (product edupression.com®) available at [ General Terms and Conditions (=T&C Patients)].

3. Service Description

3.1 The products provided by SOFY to the users currently include the digital medical device and self-help program Psychoeducation as well as the digital medical device Mood Chart and a supervised forum. Furthermore SOFY provides a cockpit (dashboard) for users and for the depression specialists. The free products currently include a specialist newsletter, certain contents of the self-help program and a simplified version of the mood chart. Further information is available on the website. Part of the contract between SOFY and the user will be the products ordered by the user during the booking process. SOFY may discontinue, limit or change the provision of the free products at any time without giving reasons.

3.2 In the relationship between SOFY and the depression specialist, the following service content applies: SOFY provides the depression specialist with a user account with a dashboard, which serves the administration of his patients, who are users of SOFY products and/or free products of SOFY. Furthermore, SOFY provides the depression specialist with access to the cockpits (dashboards) of those users who have concluded a treatment contract with the depression specialist and who have given their express consent for this access. This gives the depression specialist access to the contents of the user's cockpit (e.g. course of the course, reports, mood chart). The Depression Specialist declares that he is aware of the General Terms and Conditions (GTC) of SOFY GmbH (product edupression.com®).

4.1 The contract between SOFY and the depression specialist comes about when the depression specialist registers on the website either on his own initiative or by invitation of a user and SOFY subsequently agrees to the conclusion of the contract with the depression specialist.

During registration the user account for the depression specialist will be created. SOFY is entitled to refuse to conclude a contract with a depression specialist without giving reasons.
