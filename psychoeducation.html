edupression.com® psychoeducation increases motivation and initiates behavioral changes in those affected by the illness.

Psychoeducation - what is that?

Psychoeducation provides a patient with knowledge about your illness and consequently aims to make you an expert in your own illness. This means that they will recognize connections and initiate changes in behavior.
