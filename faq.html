What is edupression.com®?

The medical devices provided by edupression.com® were developed scientifically with a multidisciplinary team of doctors and psychologists. They are based on the most recent medical findings and contain many elements of cognitive behavioural therapy. 20 units in total are available to patients covering different topic areas. These units contain videos, information sections, quizzes and exercises. The many interactive elements and voice overs make the modules easy to manage.

These therapy units are embedded within an LMS (learning management system). This makes it possible to access the material – which is not always so simple – in an interactive way.

The edupression.com® platform enables patients to use our products together with their depression specialist. This is why numerous opportunities for interaction are provided. As well as providing support individually, groups can also be created by depression specialists and led through the modules.

edupression.com® supports the evidence-based treatment of patients who suffer from unipolar depression. Extensive reports have been made available for this purpose. In this way, edupression.com® creates a great deal of transparency for patients and their depression specialists about all phases of therapy.

Patients are also able to address questions to a team of doctors and psychologists in the edupression.com® moderated forums.
