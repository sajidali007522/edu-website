Privacy Statement

What is edupression.com®?

edupression.com® is an online platform for unipolar depressive patients and their depression specialists (doctors, psychologists, therapists) with two CE marked medical devices: the edupression.com® psychoeducation and the edupression.com® mood diagram.

Edupression.com® psychoeducation is an effective, evidence-based, online therapy program and therapy support. It contains many elements of cognitive behavior therapy and offers comprehensive information on depression.

The edupression.com® mood diagram was developed on a scientific basis and helps in the detection of disease relationships, the documentation and detection of early symptoms, as well as in achieving an optimal treatment result.

The use of edupression.com® psychoeducation or the edupression.com® mood diagram does not replace a doctor or therapist.

How does edupression.com® work?

The medical products from edupression.com® were developed on a scientific basis and with a multidisciplinary team of doctors and psychologists. They are based on the latest medical knowledge and contain many elements of cognitive behavior therapy. A total of 20 units with different subject areas are available to the patients. These units contain videos, information elements, quizzes and exercises. Many interactive elements and speakers (Voice over) facilitate the handling of the modules.

These therapy units are embedded in an LMS (learning management system), which enables playful access to a not always easy subject.
The platform of edupression.com® offers that patients use the offer together with their depression specialists. Therefore, numerous interaction options are provided. In addition to individual support, groups can be set up by the depression specialist and guided through the modules.

edupression.com® supports evidence-based therapy for patients suffering from unipolar depression. For this purpose, extensive reports are made available to the patients and depression specialists. In this way, edupression.com® creates extensive transparency for patients and their depression specialists across all phases of therapy.

Patients are also offered to address questions about depression issues to a team of doctors and psychologists in the supervised forums of edupression.com®.

How often should edupression.com® psychoeducation and the mood chart be used?

You should fill out the edupression.com® mood chart daily to achieve the desired effects. You should do psychoeducation from edupression.com® at least two to three times a week. The exercises shown there as needed. In many cases, a certain period of implementation is a prerequisite for their effectiveness. Please take enough time for the exercises and also download the summaries of the individual chapters.

If you are unable to understand the content conveyed, please skip it and discuss how to proceed with your depression specialist. You are also welcome to repeat certain content several times. All modules are available as often as you wish during an up-to-date membership. Make use of it!

Is my personal information safe?

edupression.com® attaches great importance to data protection. All data is only saved anonymously. In addition, the relevant laws on the protection of personal data set clear guidelines. The data is stored in a secure and certified data center in Germany. No data is stored on the end device.

Are the products from edupression.com® the right solution for me?

The edupression.com® psychoeducation and the edupression.com® mood diagram were developed for patients with unipolar depression. The evidence-based foundations processed here are strongly recommended by national and international guidelines (e.g. S3 guidelines or NICE guidelines) for the majority of all patients (exception: patients with severe depression and / or who were admitted to hospital).
The positive effects have been extensively described in the medical-scientific literature.

What are the risks or side effects?

We are currently not aware of any side effects. If you are overwhelmed by the wealth of information provided by edupression.com® and are disappointed, please interrupt the program and discuss how to proceed with your depression specialist.

Please be aware that edupression.com® does not replace a doctor, psychologist or therapist, but should generally be used as a complement.

Can edupression.com® be combined with medication?

Yes. Please only inform your doctor that you are using edupression.com® - also send your doctor the folder with the edupression.com® reports (including mood diagram, PHQ9, LMS, 2-back).

Can I pass on the access data to third parties?

No. Apart from the contractual provisions - all tests, reports, LMS and mood chart entries and reports are tailored to you personally. Passing on results in incorrect, unusable results.

How can I contact edupression.com®?

Is there a personal contact person I can contact?

edupression.com® is a manufacturer and supplier of medical devices. We provide you with a platform with medical devices on which you and your depression specialist will find optimal conditions to work together on your illness. You can also contact us at office@edupression.com if you have any questions .

Can edupression.com® replace therapy with a therapist or drug therapy?

No, that is not the goal of edupression.com®. We want to be the platform for patients and depression specialists (doctors, psychologists, psychotherapists), on which both should find optimal conditions and products to combat depression. Exceptions to this can be the bridging of waiting times for a therapy place or - based on international guidelines (e.g. NICE guidelines) - the implementation of computer-assisted therapy for depression complaints with a PHQ9 value less than or equal to 9.

Is it possible to arrange therapy sessions on your website or to find a suitable therapist?

First and foremost, we want to provide patients and their depression specialists with a common platform and effective medical devices in the fight against depression. For those patients who have not yet worked with a depression specialist, we offer an overview with depression specialists - doctors, psychologists, therapists. Patients are welcome to contact them. This is an additional service from edupression.com®, which is currently under construction. We do not become contractual partners and do not receive any remuneration for this.

Can I network or exchange ideas with other users?

Yes, we offer four "supervised forums". In these you can exchange information on various questions about depression (positive activities, Q&A about our modules, news about depression, questions for the edupression.com® community) with other users. The forums are moderated by doctors and psychologists from our team.

What does Evidence-Based Depression Treatment Mean?

edupression.com stands for evidence-based depression treatment, which means that we rely exclusively on scientifically and medically based knowledge and try to convey this to our patients in the context of our medical devices.

Why and which reports does edupression.com® offer for patients?

We also aim to bring more transparency to the treatment of depression. We offer patients various reports, which are summarized in a patient folder. This includes the mood chart report, the PHQ9 depression test and the 2-back test, to name a few. As a patient, you can share these reports with your depression specialist, with your family or friends and colleagues through various channels (mail, social media, ..).
These reports and tests can also be an important basis for therapy decisions - please also discuss these reports with your depression specialist.
