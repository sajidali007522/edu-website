About PHQ9 Test

There are a number of different depression tests that can be used for diagnosis. The PHQ-9 patient questionnaire is effective, as it not only measures the severity of depression, but also identifies whether a patient is actually suffering from depression in the first place.

Other popular tests such as e.g. the Beck Depression Inventory (BDI) or the Quick Inventory for Depressive Symptomatology (QIDS), on the other hand, only measure the severity of depression and/or take more time to complete. In addition, despite the very short processing time, the PHQ-9 provides enormously reliable results, which means that the test diagnostic quality criteria are very high compared to other tests.

The questions asked by the PHQ-9 are all based on the official diagnostic criteria for major depression according to the DSM-IV - the standard reference for the classification of mental illnesses.
